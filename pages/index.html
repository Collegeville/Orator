Welcome to the Collegeville Orator Project.

Orator is dedicated to promoting and teaching effective (technical) speaking and listening. You will find resources for learning the tools of technical speaking and best practices in effective speaking. While the content here is particularly focused on speaking for science, engineering and other technical fields, it can be valuable to anyone who wants to learn effective presentation skills.

Tell Us Your Story

A major focus of the Orator Project is clear speaking through the practice of effective story telling, presentation of detailed technical content and engagement in technical dialogue. Verbal communication is an essential skill for any scientific effort.

Resources

Orator provides the following instruction modules:

  • Discussions That Work
  • Presentations That Work
  • Recorded Presentations.

Authors and Contributors
